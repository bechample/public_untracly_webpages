Untracely

Redact and anonymize PDFs — entirely on your device.

What it does

Untracely lets you import PDFs, mark sensitive areas (manually or from automatic suggestions), and export clean, redacted copies. All processing happens on your iPhone or iPad — no file ever leaves your device and the app makes no network calls.

Features

  • Batch import and preview of multi-page PDFs
  • Automatic detection of names, addresses, amounts, account numbers and more
  • Redaction that removes content rather than covering it up
  • Export and share anonymized copies
