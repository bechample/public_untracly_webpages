Privacy Policy

Untracely (iOS & iPadOS) · Last updated: June 30, 2026

Data collection

Untracely does not collect or transmit your PDF data. Document processing is performed on your device.

Data stored on your device

Documents you import and the settings you make (such as the always-redact list) are stored locally in the app's storage on your device. They are removed when you delete them in the app or delete the app.

Future changes

Later versions may add features that involve data processing, such as anonymous analytics to improve the app. If so, this policy will be updated accordingly, and the App Store privacy information will reflect it.
